Feeling and thinking are both decision-making tools of judgment, intuition is more similar to taking in sensory data as it’s a function of perception: “I can infer from the presence of A, B, C, and D that there should also be X, Y, and Z.” More broadly, head (OPEN MIND) and heart (TUNED EMOTIONS) are ways to decide, the gut (FOCUSED BODY) is a way to inform.
