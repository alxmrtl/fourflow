## The 12 Flow Keys

Twelve conditions that determine whether flow is accessible. Each key names a domain of human experience and its ideal state. The science behind each key is documented in `docs/research/` — it informs how the FourFlow Agent observes and asks questions, but stays backstage.

### SELF (Coral) — Reception

- **Tuned Emotions** — Can you stay present across a range of emotional intensity without shutting down or spinning out? *Science: Window of Tolerance (Siegel) — see `docs/research/tuned-emotions/`*
- **Focused Body** — Are you in your body? Can you read its signals and let them inform your decisions? *Science: Somatic Markers (Damasio) — see `docs/research/focused-body/`*
- **Open Mind** — Can you approach what you think you know with fresh eyes, without letting expertise become rigidity? *Science: Beginner's Mind / Shoshin (Suzuki Roshi) — see `docs/research/open-mind/`*

### SPACE (Sage Green) — Transmission

- **Intentional Space** — Does your environment match what you're trying to do right now? *Science: Cathedral Effect (environmental psychology) — see `docs/research/intentional-space/`*
- **Optimized Tools** — Are your tools invisible extensions of thought, or sources of friction? *Science: Tool Embodiment (Heidegger) — see `docs/research/optimized-tools/`*
- **Feedback Systems** — Are you getting the right amount of feedback at the right frequency — not drowning, not flying blind? *Science: Goldilocks Frequency (learning science) — see `docs/research/feedback-systems/`*

### STORY (Steel Blue) — Temporal Direction

- **Generative Story** — Are you telling a version of your story that empowers you, or one that keeps you stuck? *Science: Redemptive Sequences (McAdams) — see `docs/research/generative-story/`*
- **Clear Mission** — Is your target specific, felt, and committed — not just intellectually clear but anchored in your body? *Science: Goal Clarity Theory (Locke & Latham) — see `docs/research/clear-mission/`*
- **Empowered Role** — Are you actively shaping your role, or waiting for someone to give you permission? *Science: Job Crafting (Wrzesniewski & Dutton) — see `docs/research/empowered-role/`*

### SPIRIT (Amethyst) — Timeless Direction

- **Grounding Values** — Do your daily actions match what you say matters to you? *Science: Values-Behavior Gap (Festinger) — see `docs/research/grounding-values/`*
- **Ignited Curiosity** — Is genuine curiosity pulling you forward, or have you lost the spark? *Science: Curiosity Gap (Loewenstein) — see `docs/research/ignited-curiosity/`*
- **Visualized Vision** — Does your future self feel real? Are you connected to something larger than yourself? *Science: Future Self Continuity (Hershfield) + Self-Transcendence (Maslow) — see `docs/research/visualized-vision/`*

### Layer 1: RECEPTION (SELF)
*Must be functional before Layer 2 matters.*

If your emotions are volatile, your body is ignored, and your mind is rigid — you can't receive the signals that tell you what to do next. The channel is blocked at the receiver.

### Layer 2: TRANSMISSION (SPACE)
*Must be functional before Layer 3 can land.*

Even if you're personally clear, a wrong environment, broken tools, or absent feedback means the signal gets lost in transit.

### Layer 3: DIRECTION (STORY + SPIRIT)
*Only activates fully when Layer 1 + 2 are clear.*

The Story keys navigate the temporal arc (where am I going, what's the target, do I have agency). The Spirit keys anchor to the timeless ground (what's always true, what pulls me through wonder, what's bigger than me).

## The Flow Profile: Diagnostic Levels

Each key is observed on a spectrum:

### SELF Keys

**Tuned Emotions**:
- Narrow: Easily triggered into anxiety/overwhelm OR shutdown/numbness
- Moderate: Handles normal stress but cracks under pressure
- Wide: Stays regulated through significant challenge; recovers quickly

**Focused Body**:
- Disconnected: Decisions made purely from logic; body signals ignored
- Aware: Notices body signals but overrides them with thinking
- Integrated: Body wisdom is a trusted co-pilot in decision-making

**Open Mind**:
- Rigid: Strong opinions about "how things work"; expertise as identity
- Selective: Open in some domains, closed in areas of competence
- Fluid: Holds knowledge lightly; genuinely curious about being wrong

### SPACE Keys

**Intentional Space**:
- Accidental: Works wherever; no awareness of space impact
- Partially Designed: Some intentionality but default setups dominate
- Crafted: Different spaces for different cognitive modes; environment as active tool

**Optimized Tools**:
- Fighting: Regular friction, context-switching, hunting for features
- Functional: Tools work but require conscious attention to operate
- Invisible: Tools are extensions of thought; no awareness of interface during work

**Feedback Systems**:
- Too Much: Measurement anxiety; checking metrics obsessively
- Too Little: Drift without awareness; surprises when things go wrong
- Optimal: Informed without overwhelmed; natural review rhythms

### STORY Keys

**Generative Story**:
- Contaminated: Good things ruined by bad; permanently damaged by past
- Mixed: Some redemption, some contamination depending on the story
- Redemptive: Difficulty becomes growth resource; wounds become wisdom

**Clear Mission**:
- Vague: No clear target; drifting between possibilities; or overwhelmed by too many
- Partial: Intellectually clear but not felt; or clear but not committed
- Locked: Target is specific, tangible, felt, and committed; anchored now, tied to future

**Empowered Role**:
- Passive: Accepts role as given; waits for permission to change
- Reactive: Makes adjustments when pain forces it
- Proactive: Continuously reshapes task, relational, and cognitive dimensions

### SPIRIT Keys

**Grounding Values**:
- Wide gap: Stated values bear little resemblance to daily behavior; chronic energy drain
- Aware: Knows the gap exists; working on closing it
- Closed: Daily actions are expressions of authentic values; energy flows freely

**Ignited Curiosity**:
- Closed: Bored, disengaged, or overwhelmed — no healthy pull toward learning
- Sporadic: Occasional interest but no sustained pull
- Optimal: Consistent exploration; learning is its own reward; can't stop thinking about interesting problems

**Visualized Vision**:
- Abstract: Future self feels distant; disconnected from something larger
- Thin: Intellectually connected but emotionally distant
- Real: Future self feels vivid AND connected to something bigger than self; long-term decisions feel natural

## The Three Product Layers

### Layer 1: The Flow Profile (Entry Point + Data Moat)

A diagnostic that maps someone across all four pillars and 12 Flow Keys.

**In-person delivery**: Facilitated workshops, coaching sessions, organizational diagnostics
**Digital delivery**: Self-serve assessment tool (API-first, not UI-first)
**Output**: A Flow Profile showing which layer is stuck, which keys are bottlenecks, and the specific cascade that will unlock flow

**Organizational application**:
- Maps Flow Cycle vs Anti-Flow Cycle (Clarity-Autonomy-Engagement-Renewal vs Ambiguity-Control-Withdrawal-Decline)
- 12 keys apply to TEAMS not just individuals
- Reveals organizational bottleneck layer
- Designs interventions based on stuck keys

**Revenue**: Consulting fees, organizational diagnostics, individual assessments

### Layer 2: The FourFlow Agent (Consciousness Companion)

An AI agent that knows the FourFlow framework and your Flow Profile. Not a diagnostic tool — an observer. It watches, notices patterns, and asks the right question at the right time.

**How it works**:

The agent observes your conversations, tracks your language and behavior patterns over time, and maintains an evolving understanding of where you are across all 12 keys. It doesn't quiz you or run assessments — it infers your state from how you show up.

When it notices something, it responds in plain language. Not "your Tuned Emotions score is low" but "you seem wound up — have you moved your body today?" The 12 keys are its internal map, not its vocabulary. It speaks like a wise friend who happens to understand the science of human performance.

**Core behaviors**:

1. **Observes patterns** — Tracks language, energy, behavior across conversations. Notices when you're telling a disempowering story, when you've stopped being curious, when your actions don't match what you said matters.

2. **Adapts its presence** — More proactive when it senses you need pushing; fades back when you're in flow. Has seasons — might actively check in during a rough patch, then go quiet when things are clicking. Learns YOUR rhythm.

3. **Asks pointed questions** — Simple, direct, no jargon. "What would happen if you just started?" "You've been planning for a week — what are you avoiding?" "Last time you felt this way, moving your body broke it open."

4. **Updates the Flow Profile** — Doesn't just read the profile, writes to it. Notices shifts over time. "You used to override your body signals constantly. Lately you're pausing before decisions. That's real movement."

5. **Checks alignment before action** — When other agents or tools are about to execute something significant, the FourFlow Agent can flag misalignment. "You're about to commit to this — does it match what you actually want, or is this the version of you that says yes to everything?"

**The key insight**: The agent doesn't tell you what to do. It helps you notice what's true. Then you (or your other agents) handle the doing.

**Revenue**: Individual subscription, enterprise licensing, API access

### Layer 3: The Movement (Network Effect)

Flow as movement, not monument.

**Certified FourFlow Practitioners**: Coaches, therapists, org consultants trained in the methodology. They deliver the in-person work. You train the trainers.

**The FourFlow Language**: The 12 Flow Keys become shared vocabulary. People start noticing: "I haven't moved my body all day, that's why I'm stuck" or "I keep telling this story in a way that disempowers me." Language shapes consciousness.

**Community**: People living the practice, sharing profiles, supporting each other's alignment.

**Revenue**: Certification training, recertification, community membership, tool licensing
