Concept 5 Assessment

  • Most distinctive aesthetic — looks and feels nothing like existing productivity tools
  • Everything visible at once — no navigation step, zero friction to reach any tool
  • Gradient tiles with glow make pillar colors feel alive, not just labeled
  • Ghost tiles (dashed border) signal extensibility elegantly — no "coming soon" awkwardness
  • Discovery view is portfolio-grade — communicates quality before any copy is read
  • No organizing question — user must self-direct. Misses the "meet me where I am" principle
  • Bento grid can feel arbitrary — why is FlowZone big? Needs a logic new users can sense
  • Mobile: bento grids collapse poorly — needs separate mobile layout thinking
