# "Exploding Machines" - Zeitville Media

**Author**: Paul Spencer
**Publication**: The Zeitville Chronicle (zeitvillemedia.substack.com)
**Date**: February 1, 2026

## Core Theme

Spencer examines how algorithmic systems disconnect us from spontaneity and authentic human connection, using astrological transits as a framework for understanding this cultural moment.

## Astrological Context

**Leo Full Moon** serves as the primary celestial event, positioned opposite five planets in Aquarius. Spencer describes this configuration as "an incredible line up" representing a tension between detachment and engagement.

**Planetary Placements:**
- Sun, Mercury, Venus, Mars concentrated in Aquarius
- Capricorn influence from January (now transitioning)
- Pluto in Aquarius beginning long-term transit (2024 onward, replacing 2008-2024 Capricorn placement)

## Key Arguments

Spencer contends that "algorithmic systems have hijacked our physiological limbic systems," causing people to interpret neutral social interactions through fear-based digital frameworks. He illustrates this through an anecdote about a woman mortified after a stranger approached her post-mass - her reaction filtered through social media paradigms rather than present-moment reality.

**The Glasslands Concept:** Spencer warns against smooth, friction-free digital spaces offering "infinite jest" but delivering genuine isolation and disconnection from serendipity and authentic community.

## Central Message

The Leo Full Moon encourages "spontaneous combustion" - reintroducing courage into chaotic moments. Spencer advocates reconnecting with intuition and abandoning pre-filtered social narratives that prevent genuine human encounters and organic community formation.
