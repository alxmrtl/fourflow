# Therapy Session Guidance - January 23, 2026

> **Date**: January 23, 2026
> **Context**: Preparing for psychotherapy session
> **Cosmic Weather**: Venus leading in Aquarius, planets spilling from Capricorn, icebreaker energy active

## Primary Theme: Reward-Punishment Patterns

This is THE shadow work of this moment. The planets are smashing the paradigm of "If I get love/success/rest, it's because I deserved it."

**Questions for therapist**:
- Where did I learn that love/acceptance is conditional on performance?
- What would it feel like to receive good things *without* having to earn them first?
- Where do I still run the inner script: "If I do X, then I deserve Y"?

This pattern shows up in productivity, creative work, and relationships. It's deeply embedded. Now is the cosmic time to excavate it.

---

## Secondary Theme: Rigid Patterns Ready to Shatter

Venus as icebreaker is smashing frozen stagnation. The question isn't IF patterns will break - it's whether you'll consciously participate.

**Explore**:
- What patterns feel "frozen" in your life right now? (Daily systems? Visibility? How you relate?)
- What am I protecting from change even though it's stagnant?
- What would feel like *color returning* to a monochrome area?

---

## The Depth Work: Patchwork Identity vs Squeezing Into One Box

The Patchwork Age theme says: you can be many things. Goth Friday, finance bro Monday, barefoot gardener Saturday.

**Explore**:
- Where have I squeezed my multiplicity into a single acceptable identity?
- What parts of me got "glassed over" - smoothed out to fit in?
- What texture/mystery have I lost in myself?

---

## Shadow Question to Bring

The most potent question from the current transit:

> **"Where am I still operating on reward-punishment in my relationship with myself?"**

This is the cracking ice. This is where the breakthrough lives.

---

## Energy Note

Venus leading into Aquarius says: "I don't care about acceptance, I care about alignment with my vibration."

If something comes up in session that feels rebellious, refusal-oriented, or like rejection of old paradigms - lean in. The heretic/outlier energy is supported right now. You don't need to deserve the icebreaker. Let it break.
