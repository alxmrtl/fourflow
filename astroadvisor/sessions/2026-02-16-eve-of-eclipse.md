## The Visible Thing - Defined

### The Container (Not a Script)

**Share one moment from your day that felt alive.**

- Not a FourFlow post. Not a teaching. Not content.
- One moment where you felt something real - fire, wonder, frustration, breakthrough, struggle, beauty
- Put it somewhere other people can see it

### The Rules
- **Format doesn't matter**: Sentence on X. Photo on stories. Voice note to a friend. Paragraph on Substack. Whatever is lowest friction RIGHT NOW.
- **Content doesn't matter**: "Felt invincible for 30 seconds on a run." "Stared at this design and something clicked." "Felt completely lost today."
- **No pre-scripting**: Live the day first. Notice what was alive. Share THAT. The noticing comes after the living, not before.
- **Pick the lowest-stakes place**: Where you're least worried about how it lands. That means Saturn has the least grip.

### What This Signals to Yourself
"I exist beyond my private build. My lived experience has value before it's packaged."

### The Compounding Effect
After a week, you start living differently because part of you is sensing what's alive in real time. That's the state shift. Not manufactured fire - attention turned toward what's already burning.
