# The Team Conditions Audit — B2B Offer

**Register**: Buyer · **Layer**: Diagnose (B2B wedge) → leads to Teach (team training)
**Source**: Profitable Authority synthesis, June 3, 2026 (raw briefs archived at `planning/_archive/profitable-authority-raw/`)
**Status**: Validated architecture; pre-first-paid-client

---

## Best Client

VP People, Head of People, HR Director, or L&D leader at a 100–1,000 person company dealing with a capability-output gap, burnout risk, post-RTO friction, or culture drift after growth or leadership change. They already buy nearby solutions (L&D, engagement surveys, coaching, offsites, wellbeing platforms) and are frustrated that those create language, data, or temporary energy — but not root-cause diagnosis.

**Top segments (scored)**: people leaders at mid-size companies after failed fixes (37/40) · culture rebuild after merger/RTO/leadership change (35/40) · team leads who care about wellbeing + output (31/40). **Avoid first**: individuals in 9-to-5 misalignment (29/40 — weak pricing power, drifts into career coaching).

**Positioning statement**: *I help founders and managers of capable but underperforming knowledge-work teams identify the specific condition blocking better work, so they can run a targeted intervention without wasting more time on generic culture or engagement programs.*

**The register rule in action**: never sell this as "flow." Sell it as *"why your capable team is underperforming, burning out, or disengaging — and what condition is actually causing it."* That is a business problem. FourFlow is the method, revealed after the pain is recognized.

---

## The Offer

A 90-minute paid diagnostic session.

**Core promise**: In 90 minutes, identify the most likely condition blocking your team's best work and leave with one targeted intervention you can run immediately.

**Delivery**: pre-session intake (context, symptoms, prior attempts) → live diagnostic across the four layers (presented as People · Tools & Systems · Direction · Values) → outputs.

**Included**: a named bottleneck · plain-language explanation of why it's creating friction · one recommended first intervention · one observable 30-day success indicator · same-day one-page **Team Conditions Map**.

**Not included**: culture transformation, surveys/facilitation, ongoing coaching, conflict mediation, implementation beyond the first action.

**Pricing ladder**: $250–$300 first three (explicitly tied to case study/testimonial) → $400–$600 first public version → $750–$1,000 after one strong case study → $1,500–$5,000 engagement with implementation offer behind it.

## Messaging Rules

**Primary message**: I help founders and people leaders diagnose why a capable team is producing flat work, so they know what to change first before spending money on another engagement, culture, or training initiative.

**Lead with** (recognition language): capable team, flat output · "the engagement score went up, the work did not change" · diagnosis before program · culture is the residue, not the root cause · strong people rarely leave for the reason in the exit interview.

**Never lead with**: flow state · consciousness · holistic · peak performance · transform · alignment (untied to an observable problem) · Self/Space/Story/Spirit before pain recognition · meaningful work as primary hook. Don't sound like a wellness consultant or the engagement industry. Don't attack surveys/offsites — the stronger position: *useful tools fail when deployed before diagnosis.*

**Proof posture (honest)**: no paid organizational case study yet — say so. Strongest current proof is process + artifact: documented framework, large research compendium, working tools, live intake pipeline, repeated diagnostic practice (/align sessions). Never claim team outcomes until they exist.
