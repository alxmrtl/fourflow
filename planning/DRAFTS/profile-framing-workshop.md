# Profile Framing Workshop
*Workshopping metaphor alternatives to the signal/reception/transmission language in the Flow Archetype Profile prompt.*

**Date:** 2026-03-03

---

## The Problem

The current profile prompt uses signal/transmission language — "reception," "amplifying or muffling signal," "deepest channel." This is a radio engineering metaphor. It's technically precise but creates a gap: people don't experience their inner life as a signal chain. They experience it as how they feel, what's around them, where they're going, and why it matters.

---

## Four Framings

### Overview

| Version | Dimension Labels | Feel |
|---|---|---|
| **A — Conditions** | Inner / Outer / Directional / Core Conditions | Clinical, practical, clear |
| **B — Water System** | The Source / Channel / Current / Ocean | Organic, poetic, metaphorical |
| **C — State/Setup/Arc/Why** | Your State / Setup / Arc / Why | Performance, direct, universal |
| **Hybrid** | Your State / Setup / Arc / Why + plain questions | Best of each |

## Notes

**A** — Most readable and functional. "Inner/Outer/Directional/Core Conditions" is immediately clear. Risks feeling operational rather than personal.

**B** — Strongest metaphor coherence (maps naturally to the product name "flow"). "The Source / Channel / Current / Ocean" requires more orientation for a first-timer than C. Best dimension: SPIRIT as The Ocean.

**C** — Most universally resonant. Anyone who has ever performed, created, or competed already thinks in State/Setup/Arc/Why — clicks without explanation. Best dimension: SPIRIT as Your Why.

**Hybrid** — Recommended direction. Uses C's direct subheader questions (making each dimension immediately practical), B's organic prose quality in summaries, and A's plain-language precision at the key level. The archetype paragraph is the cleanest version — drops "signal/frequencies/channel" entirely while staying metaphorically rich. Key change from original: *"you receive signal fast"* → *"your state runs hot"* — lands in the body instead of the radio tower.

**The single most important line to change in the original prompt:**
> *"You receive signal fast"* — first line of the SELF summary, most technically-feeling phrase in the profile.
