# To Know or To Do: Timing & Awareness-Intent-Creativeness (AIC)

> **Author**: Ahmad Hijazi · **Type**: article

## Core Argument

The central tension of effective action is timing: when do you acquire more knowledge versus act on what you have? Hijazi proposes the AIC triple helix (Awareness → Intent → Creativeness) with the heuristic: "have the courage to take some risk, once you know enough" — where "enough" is the point where more information costs more than it reduces risk.
