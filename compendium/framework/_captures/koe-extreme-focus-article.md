# Full guide: how to unlock extreme focus on command

> **Author**: Dan Koe · **Type**: article

## Core Argument

Focus is not a character trait — it is a calibration outcome determined by the ratio between challenge and skill, the vividness of stakes, and the degree to which motivation is intrinsically structured. A practical protocol (vision/anti-vision → goal hierarchy → project-based work → lever-moving tasks) creates the conditions for deep work within even a one-hour daily window.
