# PERMISSIONMAXX: Everything Is a Permission Slip

> **Author**: Jimmy Edgar · **Type**: x-post

## Core Argument

Everything external — tools, rituals, gurus, techniques — is a permission slip that lets you access internal capacity you already have. The real practice is recognizing you are the permission, then following your excitement with zero insistence on outcome while acting from baseline intrinsic worth.

## Standout Quotes

> "The crystal doesn't heal you. The tarot doesn't predict you. The ritual doesn't transform you. YOU do all of it. The props are just permission slips to let yourself do what you were always capable of."

> "Follow your highest excitement, to the best of your ability, with zero insistence on a particular outcome." — Bashar's Formula

> "WORTHINESS = EXISTENCE. If you exist, you're worthy."

> "You're not climbing toward the vision. The vision is descending toward you, and you just need to clear the landing pad."

> "Act because you're already whole and your actions are overflow, not compensation."
