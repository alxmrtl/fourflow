# Generative Story

> The narrative layer — the ongoing story you inhabit about who you are, where you're going, and what obstacles mean. It determines whether challenge advances the plot or proves you wrong.

## Why It Enables Flow

Flow requires a sense of forward movement and meaning. The narrative frame you're operating inside shapes whether challenge is experienced as engagement or threat. A generative story makes struggle purposeful — every obstacle is a plot beat, every setback is a scene, and the protagonist is the one who acts. In this frame, the friction of the struggle phase becomes interesting rather than alarming.

The failure mode is a degenerative narrative: setbacks accumulate as evidence of inadequacy, obstacles prove the mission is wrong, and the protagonist waits for conditions to improve before acting. In this frame, flow becomes structurally inaccessible — not because conditions are wrong, but because the interpretive layer converts every signal into a reason to withdraw.
