# Recovery Accounting

> A weekly demand-vs-recovery tracking practice that prevents cognitive depletion from compounding across sessions — applying athletic periodization to knowledge work.

## Mechanism
Energy sufficiency is not just about inputs (sleep, food, movement) but about the relationship between demand and recovery. High-output flow sessions deplete cognitive resources that require time to replenish. Without accounting for this, sessions degrade across a week even with good individual-night sleep — because daily recovery cannot keep pace with daily demand. Motivation lags energy depletion by ~24–48 hours, which means the collapse often appears to come from nowhere. Athletes understand periodization — alternating high-demand and recovery phases; knowledge workers rarely apply this framework. The result is accumulated cognitive debt that masquerades as motivation problems, creative blocks, or burnout.

## Protocol

**Daily tracking (5 minutes)**:
1. Rate cognitive demand (1–5): How much did today's work draw on peak prefrontal function? Deep creative work = 4–5; admin, email, routine tasks = 1–2.
2. Rate recovery inputs (1–5): Average of sleep quality + duration, exercise, nutrition quality, non-screen rest time.

**Weekly review (Sunday)**:
3. Track the running balance: demand total vs. recovery total for the week.
4. If demand exceeded recovery for 3+ consecutive days: schedule a rest or light day the following week regardless of how motivated you feel. Motivation lags depletion.
5. **Rest days are not wasted**: Consolidation of complex material, creative recombination, and motivation restoration happen primarily during down-regulation — not during active work.

**Signs that the balance has already tipped**:
- Sessions feel harder than the challenge level warrants
- Creative output has reduced quality for 2+ consecutive days
- Low motivation despite apparent interest in the work

## When to Use
As an ongoing weekly practice, not as a response to crisis. Review Sunday; plan Monday. The system's value is preventive: catching accumulated debt before it manifests as collapse.

## Key Insight
> "You cannot debt-finance flow. Each hour of peak cognitive output draws from a reserve that refills only with real recovery."
