# Sleep Debt Audit

> A structured self-assessment to detect whether chronic sleep insufficiency is narrowing baseline cognitive bandwidth — and what to do about it.

## Mechanism
Sleep debt is cumulative and non-obvious. Most people operating on 6 hours of sleep per night believe they have adapted; cognitive testing shows they have not — they've adapted to feeling impaired. During slow-wave sleep, brain ATP levels surge, replenishing the energy currency that waking cognition depleted (Porkka-Heiskanen et al., 2010). REM sleep enhances creative problem-solving by 40% on tasks primed before sleep (Walker, 2017). Sleep debt is not just fatigue — it is depletion of the substrate that makes high-bandwidth thinking possible.

## Protocol

1. **Run the 10-day baseline**: For 10 days, sleep without an alarm if possible. Record natural wake time each morning. By day 7–10, the numbers stabilize around your true recovery amount (debt paid off, no longer masking it).
2. **Check two indicators of debt**:
   - Falling asleep within 5 minutes of lying down → significant sleep debt
   - Sleeping significantly longer on weekends vs. weekdays → chronic shortfall
3. **Set a target bedtime**: Calculate backward from natural wake time to allow 7.5–9 hours in bed. For most adults this means moving bedtime 60–90 minutes earlier than current.
4. **Protect REM**: The final 90 minutes of sleep (typically 6–7.5 hours in) are disproportionately REM-dense. An alarm cutting this window truncates creative consolidation. Where possible, use light alarm or natural wake.
5. **Re-run when output drops**: When creative quality declines, motivation is low, or focus sessions feel unusually hard to enter — run the audit before assuming motivational or skill causes.

## When to Use
Any time creative output quality drops, motivation is consistently low, or focus sessions feel harder to enter than expected. Also as a quarterly baseline check regardless of felt performance — sleep debt is under-perceived by design.

## Key Insight
> "You cannot think your way out of sleep debt. Every flow intervention stacks on top of sleep. Below it, everything is penalty mode."
