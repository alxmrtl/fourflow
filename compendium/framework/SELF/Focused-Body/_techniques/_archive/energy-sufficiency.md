# Energy Sufficiency

> Ensures metabolic and nervous system readiness.

## Mechanism

Flow is metabolically expensive. The neural state of flow — transient hypofrontality, dopamine release, heightened sensory processing — requires a nervous system with enough reserve capacity to support sustained high-performance demand. When metabolic reserves are depleted through sleep debt, poor nutrition, or insufficient recovery, the brain's first defense is to reduce cognitive ambition: slower processing, narrower attention, reduced working memory, lower motivation. The aperture narrows not because of emotional patterns but because there is literally insufficient fuel to keep it open. Sleep, nutrition, and movement interact non-additively — optimizing all three produces cognitive performance significantly above what any single factor predicts. Energy sufficiency is the physical prerequisite for the lens to function.

## Techniques

- [[sleep-debt-audit]] — structured self-assessment to detect chronic sleep insufficiency; establishes the true recovery amount and protects REM-rich sleep windows
- [[stable-glucose-protocol]] — meal timing and composition that maintains consistent brain fuel across a session; caffeine timing for clean adenosine management
- [[movement-primer]] — 20 minutes of moderate aerobic exercise 30–60 minutes before deep work; manufactures the BDNF and neurotransmitter conditions for flow
- [[recovery-accounting]] — weekly demand-vs-recovery tracking; applies athletic periodization to knowledge work to prevent cumulative cognitive depletion

## Recall

**Hook:** You cannot think your way out of sleep debt — every flow intervention stacks on top of sleep; below it, everything is penalty mode.
**Mechanism:** Flow is metabolically expensive; sleep debt depletes brain ATP and triggers compensatory cortisol, pushing arousal past the optimal zone where flow lives — the aperture narrows not from emotion but from insufficient fuel.
**Anchor:** The three inputs stack: sleep (protect REM, 7.5–9 hours), nutrition (low-glycemic pre-session meal, delay coffee 90 min), movement (20 min aerobic, 30–60 min before session). Any one neglected degrades the others.
