# Mind-Body Coherence

> Align intention with physical execution.

## Why It Enables Flow

When what the mind intends and what the body is doing are misaligned — thinking about one thing while the body is doing another, or holding tension in the body that contradicts the calm focus the work requires — a split forms that consumes attentional bandwidth reconciling the two signals. Mind-body coherence is the closing of this gap: intention and execution move as one system, reducing the internal friction that slows entry into and maintenance of flow.
