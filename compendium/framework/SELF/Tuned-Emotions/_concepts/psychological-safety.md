# Psychological Safety

> Removes fear of judgment during execution.

## Why It Enables Flow

When we fear judgment — from others or from ourselves — the system shifts into protective mode: the heart closes, patterns activate, and attention fragments into monitoring. Fear of judgment activates the amygdala's threat-detection circuitry, which suppresses prefrontal cortex activity — the same prefrontal relaxed-alert engagement that characterizes flow (Dietrich, 2003). The deeper insight is that psychological safety isn't primarily about external conditions: it's about the internal state of the energetic heart. When the heart is open, its natural qualities (trust, openness, love, gratitude) create an internal foundation that doesn't depend on external validation. You can take creative risks because your well-being isn't contingent on outcomes. The heart's factory setting is open — the work is restoration, not construction.
