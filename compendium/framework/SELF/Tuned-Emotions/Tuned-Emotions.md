# Tuned Emotions

> The emotional calibration layer — using affect signals to navigate toward optimal arousal for flow.

## Why It Enables Flow

Emotions are real-time readouts of the challenge-skills ratio. Anxiety means the challenge exceeds your current capacity; boredom means your capacity exceeds the challenge. When you can read these signals accurately — and act on them — your emotional system becomes a self-correcting navigation instrument rather than a source of interference.

The failure mode isn't feeling emotion. It's misreading it: treating anxiety as evidence of inadequacy rather than a calibration cue, or treating boredom as acceptable rather than a signal to raise the stakes. Tuned Emotions closes this loop.
