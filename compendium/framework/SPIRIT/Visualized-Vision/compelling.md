# Vision Priming

> Preload direction before action begins.

## Why It Enables Flow

Vision provides the long-range attractor that orients action. Without direction, attention scatters — everything competes equally, and every fork requires fresh evaluation. With vision primed, the system has a coordinate that makes moment-to-moment decisions effortless: relevant opportunities become obvious; irrelevant distractions fade. But there's a critical quality variable: a scarcity-derived vision (organized around escaping fear) keeps the threat-detection system partly activated even while pursuing it, creating a mixed state that never fully resolves. Abundance-native vision creates clean pull — complete engagement without the background threat-signal.

## Recall
**Hook**: A scarcity-derived vision pulls you toward the goal and away from fear simultaneously — that dual orientation never fully resolves.
**Mechanism**: Vision is a coordinate in a branching structure; priming aims the flashlight at it, turning each fork into a binary (toward/away) and making navigation effortless — but only if the vision is abundance-native; scarcity visions keep threat-detection partly activated.
**Anchor**: Anti-Vision Contrast Primer — write what you don't want (specific, until genuine discomfort); from that state, write the positive vision; then apply the abundance test: "If all scarcity were removed, what would I actually do?" What survives both steps is closer to the real vision.
