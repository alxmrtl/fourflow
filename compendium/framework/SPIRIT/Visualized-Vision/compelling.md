# Compelling
*The Restore quality of Visualized Vision*

## What it is

When this quality is present, the session has a coordinate before it starts. The vision has been activated — not just recalled, but felt — so that moment-to-moment decisions have a reference point. Relevant opportunities are obvious; irrelevant distractions fade. The navigation is effortless because the destination is already loaded. When absent, attention scatters: everything competes equally, every fork requires fresh evaluation, and the energy that should go into the work goes into orienting. The restore move is priming — a brief but genuine reconnection to the vision that preloads direction before action begins. There is also a critical quality variable: visions organized around escaping something (fear, inadequacy) keep the threat-detection system partly activated even while pursuing them. Abundance-native vision creates clean pull.

## Science

Future self-continuity research (Hershfield et al., 2011) shows that vividly imagining a future self increases the degree to which that future self feels continuous with the present — which in turn increases willingness to invest in it. Priming works through this identification mechanism: the clearer and more felt the future vision, the more the present moment is experienced as a trajectory toward it rather than an isolated event.

Positive affect broadening (Fredrickson, 2001) demonstrates that abundance-oriented states expand the attentional field — increasing creative problem-solving, peripheral awareness, and engagement — while threat-narrowing (fear, scarcity orientation) constricts it. A compelling vision organized around aspiration activates broaden-and-build; a vision organized around fear-flight keeps threat-detection partly active, creating a mixed motivational state that never fully resolves.

Pull-motivation theory (Higgins, 2000) distinguishes promotion focus (approaching desired end-states) from prevention focus (avoiding undesired end-states). Promotion-focused motivation generates more creative engagement, greater persistence through difficulty, and higher flow frequency — while prevention focus produces vigilant, cautious effort that is incompatible with the openness flow requires.

## Recall

**Hook**: A scarcity-derived vision pulls you toward the goal and away from fear simultaneously — that dual orientation never fully resolves.
**Mechanism**: Primed vision converts each decision fork from a fresh evaluation into a binary (toward/away from the destination); abundance-native vision activates clean pull without background threat-detection; scarcity-derived vision sustains a mixed state.
**Anchor**: Anti-Vision Contrast Primer — write what you don't want (specific, until genuine discomfort); then write the positive vision from that state; then the abundance test: "If all scarcity were removed, what would I actually do?" What survives both steps is closer to the real vision.
