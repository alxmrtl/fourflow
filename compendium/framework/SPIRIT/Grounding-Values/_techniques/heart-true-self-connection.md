# Heart–True Self Connection

> A brief protocol for accessing the True Self's guidance on alignment — bypassing the thinking mind's conditioned patterns when analytical assessment isn't producing clarity.

## Mechanism
The thinking mind operates from patterns and conditioning; it often produces more noise than signal when the question is about authentic alignment. The True Self (SOUL) — accessed through the heart — sees beyond these patterns. Establishing the heart–SOUL connection provides access to a quality of knowing the analytic mind can't reach: not logical analysis, but direct recognition of what's actually aligned.

## Protocol

1. Find a calm, settled state — use breath as an anchor (box breath, or the simple I-am-here-now breath)
2. Shift attention from the head to the heart space in the chest
3. Connect through intention and feeling — notice the heart's qualities (warmth, openness, stillness, or whatever is present)
4. Invite connection to the True Self/SOUL — this is an act of opening, not forcing
5. From this unified state, pose the alignment question clearly
6. Remain open to what emerges — it may come as feeling, voice, image, or direct knowing rather than logical analysis

The protocol can be done in under a minute (quick check) or extended into a longer session when the question is significant.

## When to Use
When the internal integrity check or pushed/pulled assessment isn't producing clarity. Before major decisions or commitments when the thinking mind is generating conflicting signals. When you sense that analytical reasoning is running a conditioned pattern rather than accessing truth. Not needed for every decision — most alignment is accessible through the simpler checks.
