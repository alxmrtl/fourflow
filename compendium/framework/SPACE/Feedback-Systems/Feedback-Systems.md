# Feedback Systems

> The signal intelligence layer — designing workflows that generate fast, clear, actionable signals so the brain can self-correct in real time rather than navigating blind.

## Why It Enables Flow

Flow requires immediate feedback. Without it, the brain can't calibrate challenge level and loses the self-correcting quality that makes action feel effortless. When feedback is fast and task-relevant, attention can stay on the work itself — no need to monitor internally, second-guess, or pause for assessment. The work steers itself.

The failure mode is delayed or absent feedback: quarterly reviews instead of real-time signals, metrics that measure ego instead of task, and feedback so infrequent that course-correction is always too late. The brain falls back on anxiety — monitoring itself rather than navigating the task. Flow becomes inaccessible because the steering mechanism has been removed.
