# Immediate Feedback

> Shortens the loop between action and information.

## Why It Enables Flow

The shorter the interval between action and information, the more the brain can treat the task as a real-time conversation rather than a delayed judgment. Long feedback loops break immersion and allow the mind to wander — there is nothing to attend to in the gap. Immediate feedback keeps attention anchored to the present action by making every move consequential at the moment it's made.
