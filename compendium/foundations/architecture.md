# FourFlow Architecture

> Five levels. Five principles. One fractal. The complete map from SOUL to actionable technique.

---

## The Model

FourFlow is a consciousness alignment framework. It does not add capacity — it removes what obstructs access to capacity already present. Every level of the architecture is a removal operation: clearing what prevents the light from passing through.

The framework has five levels. Each level is governed by one of the Five First Principles. Each level contains the one below it. All levels are simultaneously active in every moment — no level precedes another in lived experience. Sequential reading is a diagnostic tool, not the operating architecture.

---

## The Five-Level Stack

```
SOUL                      — Default Is Open
  └── Dimension ×4        — Direction Precedes Force
        └── State ×12     — Coherence Is the Mechanism
              ├── Restore Quality ×12    — Default Is Open (echo)
              ├── Maintain Quality ×12   — Loops Close Gaps
              └── Concentrate Quality ×12 — Compression Concentrates Signal
                    ├── Technique   (the HOW — atomic protocol)
                    └── Concept     (the WHY — external science)
```

## Level 1: SOUL

**Principle**: Default Is Open

SOUL is the light source. Infinite, unchanging, always on. It does not need to be created or earned — only uncovered. SOUL perceives the totality of experience simultaneously: no aperture restriction, no sequential processing, no time as cost. What every tradition calls "the eternal now" is SOUL's default mode of perception.

The four Dimensions are SOUL's light expressed through a finite nervous system. The heart is the iris — the aperture mechanism between SOUL's infinite light and lived experience. When patterns accumulate (conditioning, armor, contractions), the iris narrows and the light passes through in fragments, generating what we experience as time, effort, and separation. Clearing patterns = widening the aperture = approaching SOUL's natural state.

FourFlow does not develop SOUL. It maintains the lens.

**See**: `compendium/framework/_concepts/soul.md` · `compendium/foundations/time-as-aperture.md`

## Level 2: Dimensions ×4

**Principle**: Direction Precedes Force

The four Dimensions are the four orientations of consciousness — the directions SOUL's light can point. They are simultaneously active in every moment. No Dimension is more fundamental than another; none precedes another. They are the four faces of the same mandala.

| Dimension | Orientation | Function | Question |
|-----------|-------------|----------|----------|
| **SELF** | Inward | Reception — can I receive the signal? | Am I clear enough to take this in? |
| **SPACE** | Outward | Transmission — does my environment carry the signal? | Is my context amplifying or degrading signal? |
| **STORY** | Temporal | Direction across time — where am I in the arc? | What am I building toward? |
| **SPIRIT** | Eternal | Direction beyond time — what is always true? | What doesn't change regardless of conditions? |

**Simultaneous, not sequential**: The diagnostic sequence (SPIRIT → STORY → SELF → SPACE) is a repair heuristic, not the operating architecture. All four Dimensions are always active. You are never "in" only one — you are always in all four simultaneously, at varying levels of coherence.

**See**: `compendium/framework/[SELF|SPACE|STORY|SPIRIT]/`

## Level 3: States ×12

**Principle**: Coherence Is the Mechanism

Each Dimension contains three States — named coherent configurations that describe what optimal function looks like within that Dimension. A State is not a thing you do; it is a configuration you can be in. Achieving a State means its constituent Qualities are active and aligned.

Flow is not a separate achievement — it is what emerges when all twelve States are simultaneously coherent.

The State names are FourFlow's primary vocabulary. They are not jargon for external use; they are the internal map the FourFlow Agent uses to observe, diagnose, and ask the right question.

| Dimension | States |
|-----------|--------|
| **SELF** | Tuned Emotions · Focused Body · Open Mind |
| **SPACE** | Intentional Space · Optimized Tools · Feedback Systems |
| **STORY** | Generative Story · Clear Mission · Empowered Role |
| **SPIRIT** | Grounding Values · Ignited Curiosity · Visualized Vision |

**See**: Each State has its own directory within its Dimension folder.

## Level 4: Qualities ×36

**Principles**: Default Is Open · Loops Close Gaps · Compression Concentrates Signal

Every State is composed of exactly three Qualities. The same three principle-types apply to every State — creating a universal diagnostic shorthand.

| Quality Type | Principle | The question it answers |
|-------------|-----------|------------------------|
| **Restore** | Default Is Open | What does returning this State to its natural configuration look like? |
| **Maintain** | Loops Close Gaps | What feedback gap must stay closed for this State to hold? |
| **Concentrate** | Compression Concentrates Signal | What does maximum signal density look like here? |

**Diagnostic shorthand**: If a State is degraded, identify which Quality type is missing.
- Missing **Restore** quality → subtraction problem. Something is present that shouldn't be. Remove it.
- Missing **Maintain** quality → open loop. A feedback gap is costing bandwidth. Close it.
- Missing **Concentrate** quality → diffusion problem. Signal is scattered or diluted. Compress it.

## Level 5: Techniques

The leaf level. These are not FourFlow-proprietary — they are the practical layer that makes each Quality actionable.

**Techniques** — the HOW. Atomic, repeatable protocols with specific steps, concrete enough to follow without reading the source. Each Technique addresses one Quality within one State. Lives in `_techniques/` within each State's directory.

| Level | Principle | Role at this level |

| SOUL | Default Is Open | Ontological ground — consciousness is whole by default |
| Dimension | Direction Precedes Force | Each Dimension IS a specific orientation of consciousness |

## Two Reading Modes

### Operational (default): Simultaneous

All four Dimensions are active in every moment. All twelve States are present at some level of coherence. Flow emerges when all twelve are simultaneously coherent. This is the mandala model: SOUL at center, four Dimensions as the cardinal orientations, twelve States arrayed at their intersections. You are always in all of it at once — the question is only which States are currently coherent and which are not.

### Diagnostic (repair): Sequential

When something is off, the principles have a natural repair sequence: check Direction first (is SPIRIT clear?), then Coherence (is STORY aligned?), then Loops (is SELF's feedback running?), then Compression (is SPACE's signal concentrated?). In practice: SPIRIT → STORY → SELF → SPACE.

This sequence is a heuristic, not architecture. Don't confuse the repair manual with the machine.
