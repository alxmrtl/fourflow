## The Holistic Metaphor: Keys, Gates, Aperture

Each word does exactly one job:

- **SOUL** is the light. Always on, never developed — only uncovered (`soul.md`, `time-as-aperture.md` already carry this).
- The **four Dimensions** are the four faces the light shines through: inward (SELF), outward (SPACE), through time (STORY), beyond time (SPIRIT).
- Each face has three **gates**; each **Key** opens one. Twelve gates, twelve keys, three per face. Conditioning, noise, and accumulated patterns rust gates shut; the work of FourFlow is turning the keys.
- The **aperture** is the aggregate: how many gates stand open is how much of SOUL's light gets through. Not a mechanism you operate directly — the *result* of the keys you've turned.
- **Flow is full aperture** — all twelve gates open, the light passing undivided. Not a peak experience added to life; the default that was always underneath.
- **Qualities** are how a key turns: clear what's jamming the gate (Restore), keep its hinge moving (Maintain), open it all the way (Concentrate).
- **Techniques** are the hand on the key — the specific motions.

So: **Keys open gates → open gates widen the aperture → full aperture is flow.** Each level keeps its native verb: keys *unlock*, gates *open*, the aperture *widens*, flow *arrives*.

Worth noting, not overclaiming: a walled structure with four faces and three gates per face is an old archetype (twelve city gates facing the four directions; the zodiac's four elements × three modalities). The geometry was already in the framework — the metaphor just lets it show.

## Keys, Not States — The Reconciliation

The March 2026 refactor renamed the 12 from "Flow Keys" to "States." The refactor's *structural* insight stands (each of the twelve is a coherent configuration, composed of exactly 3 Qualities). The *name* is reverted:

- **Key** is the canonical level name. Keys act — they unlock, open, focus. "State" described the destination but not the function, and named neither the work nor the leverage.
- **Each Key names the state it opens.** "Tuned Emotions" is the key you turn *and* the configuration that results. When precision requires the distinction, write: *the Tuned Emotions key* (the condition you diagnose and train) vs. *a tuned-emotions state* (the configuration when it's open). In ordinary writing, "Key" carries both.
- "State" remains available as a plain English word ("your current state") — it is no longer a level name and is never capitalized as one.
- Public long form: **Flow Keys** ("the 12 Flow Keys"). Short form everywhere else: **Keys**.

### Profile prose framing (decided — from profile-framing workshop)

Profile documents address the four Dimensions with these subheads and plain questions:

- **SELF — Your State**: *Are you actually available for this work?*
- **SPACE — Your Setup**: *Is your environment working for you, or are you working around it?*
- **STORY — Your Arc**: *Do you know where you're going, and do you own the journey?*
- **SPIRIT — Your Why**: *Is there something real beneath the arc that makes it worth doing?*

No radio-engineering language in anything a client reads.
