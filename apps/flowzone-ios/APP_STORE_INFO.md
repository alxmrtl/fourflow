### Description (4000 chars max)
```
FlowZone helps you achieve deep focus and flow state through structured work sessions, breathwork exercises, binaural beats, and the Four Pillars framework.

FOUR PILLARS OF FLOW:
• Spirit: Define your values and vision to fuel intrinsic motivation
• Story: Set meaningful goals that pull you forward
• Space: Optimize your environment for focus
• Self: Take focused action with intention

KEY FEATURES:

Focus Timer
Customizable focus sessions (25, 50, or 90 minutes) designed around the science of flow states. The timer recognizes the Struggle Phase—the first 25% of any session where starting is hardest—and provides ambient encouragement to help you push through.

Focus Reps
Track every moment you choose focus over distraction. Each time distraction pulls at your attention but you return to focus, press the button. This makes the invisible work of maintaining focus visible and measurable.

Binaural Beats
10 Hz alpha wave binaural beats to induce flow state (headphones recommended). Smooth fade-in, adjustable volume, easily toggled on or off.

Breathwork Integration
7 different breathing patterns to transition from scattered thinking to focused presence:
• Box Breathing (4-4-4-4)
• 4-7-8 Relaxation
• Coherent Breathing
• Energizing patterns
• And more

Daily Container
Plan 1-5 focus sessions per day. See your progress at a glance and build a consistent practice.

Task Pipeline
Organize your work across goals. Connect focus sessions to meaningful objectives and track progress over time.

Stats & Review
Track your focus sessions, flow experiences, rep counts, and session history. See your growth over time.

PRIVACY FIRST
All data is stored locally on your device. No account required. No cloud sync. No data collection. Your focus journey stays with you.

Built on the FourFlow framework for sustainable high performance and flow state cultivation.
```

### Demo Account
Not required - app uses local storage only, no account system.

### Demo Instructions
```
1. Launch app and complete onboarding (select values, add a goal)
2. Add a task from the Flow screen by tapping the + button
3. Enable Binaural Beats in Settings > SETTINGS panel (optional, requires headphones)
4. Tap START to begin a focus session
5. Use the Rep button during focus to track distraction resistance
6. Complete session and view stats in Review tab
7. Try breathwork by enabling it before a focus session
```
