Pre-Session Clearance
The Protocol
01Pause 3–5 minutes before your session begins. Do not open any tools yet.
02Name what you're carrying — any unresolved emotion, worry, or background distraction.
03Give it permission to exist. Acknowledge without suppressing or resolving.
04Set it aside with intention: "I'll return to this after."
05Take 3 slow exhales. Each exhale signals the nervous system to transition into focus mode.
When to Use
Before any focused work session, especially creative or deep work.
After emotionally charged conversations or unexpected events.
When you notice restlessness or resistance at the moment of starting.
Key Insight
The goal isn't to eliminate emotion — it's to prevent it from bleeding into focus time. Emotion acknowledged is emotion that doesn't compete for bandwidth.
