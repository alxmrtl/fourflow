# FourFlow Voice Examples

*A practical guide to writing in the FourFlow voice*

## Headlines & Taglines

### Hero Headlines (What Works)

**Strong:**
```
Flow is not something you force.
The struggle phase is built in.
Stop climbing. Start centering.
Four dimensions. One state.
The invisible work, made visible.
Focus is a muscle. This is the gym.
```

**Weak (avoid):**
```
Unlock Your Full Potential Today!
The Revolutionary Focus System
Harness the Power of Flow States
Embark on Your Productivity Journey
Transform Your Life in Just 5 Minutes
```

### Subheadlines (What Works)

**Strong:**
```
When Self, Space, Story, and Spirit align, flow emerges naturally.
You don't force presence. You create the conditions for it.
Built for the ones who know there's another way.
```

**Weak (avoid):**
```
Our cutting-edge platform leverages the latest science...
In today's fast-paced world, staying focused is crucial...
Join thousands who have already transformed their lives...
```

## Body Copy Patterns

### Opening a Page or Section

**Strong approach:**
```
You've felt it before.

That state where time bends. Where the work flows through you
instead of being forced from you. Where you look up and three
hours have passed like three minutes.

This is flow. And it's not random.
```

**Weak approach:**
```
Welcome to FourFlow! In today's ever-evolving world, focus
has become more important than ever. Our innovative platform
is designed to help you unlock your hidden potential and
embark on a transformative journey toward peak productivity.
```

### Explaining a Feature

**Strong:**
```
Focus Reps

Every time distraction pulls at you, you have a choice.
Give in, or return.

Press the button when you return. That's one rep.

The invisible work—the countless micro-decisions to stay
present—finally has a number. Not to judge you. To show
you what you're actually doing.
```

**Weak:**
```
Focus Rep Tracking System

Our revolutionary Focus Rep feature harnesses the power of
behavioral science to help you build sustainable focus habits.
Simply tap the button each time you notice distraction and
choose to refocus. This cutting-edge approach helps you
unlock new levels of productivity.
```

### Describing the Pillars

**Strong:**
```
SELF — What's happening inside you right now

Body, mind, emotions. The instrument you play your life through.
When it's tuned, everything sounds better.

SPACE — What surrounds you

Your environment isn't neutral. It's either supporting your
focus or stealing it. Every object, every notification, every
open tab is voting on what you pay attention to.

STORY — What you're building toward

Without direction, even perfect focus goes nowhere. Your
mission, your narrative, your sense of why this matters—this
is what turns presence into progress.

SPIRIT — What drives you underneath it all

Values. Vision. The curiosity that pulls you forward. The
deepest answer to "why bother?" When this is clear, the
other pillars align naturally.
```

## Error Messages & Edge Cases

### Empty States

**Strong:**
```
Nothing here yet.

When you complete your first focus session,
it'll show up right here.
```

**Weak:**
```
Oops! It looks like you haven't completed any
sessions yet. Don't worry - your transformation
journey is just beginning!
```

### Error Messages

**Strong:**
```
That didn't work. Try again?
```

```
Connection lost. Your data is safe—we'll sync
when you're back online.
```

```
Something went wrong on our end.
We're looking into it.
```

**Weak:**
```
Oops! An error occurred. Please try again later.
We apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused.
```

### Loading States

**Strong:**
```
Preparing your space...
```

```
One moment.
```

```
[No text, just the breathing animation]
```

**Weak:**
```
Please wait while we load your personalized dashboard!
Hang tight - good things are coming!
```

## Key Phrases to Use

**The FourFlow lexicon:**

```
"the struggle phase" — the hard beginning (always honor it)
"focus reps" — the returns to attention
"the four pillars" — Self, Space, Story, Spirit
"flow state" — when effort and ease merge
"the invisible work" — what focus actually requires
"presence practice" — what we're doing here
"navigation mode / focus mode" — the two states
```

**Tone-setting phrases:**

```
"not a hack—a practice"
"built for the long game"
"the work that matters"
"returning is the skill"
"you already know this"
"it's supposed to feel hard at first"
```

## The Final Test

Before publishing any copy, read it out loud and ask:

1. Does this sound like a human who has actually struggled with focus?
2. Would I be embarrassed to say this to a friend?
3. Is there a single AI-detector word in here? (delve, unlock, leverage, etc.)
4. Am I trying to impress or trying to help?
5. Does this have texture, or is it smooth corporate nothing?

If you hesitate on any of these, revise until you don't.

---

*Write like you're leaving a note for someone you care about, not pitching to strangers.*
