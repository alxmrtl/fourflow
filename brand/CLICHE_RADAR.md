## Visual Cliches (Red Flags)

### AI-Generated Imagery Tells

- Hands with wrong finger counts
- Skin that's too smooth/waxy
- Lighting that doesn't match environment
- "Perfect" symmetry in faces
- Generic attractive people with no personality
- Impossible architectural spaces
- Objects that melt into each other at edges

**The Test:** Zoom in on hands, teeth, ears, hair edges. AI still struggles here.

## Verbal Cliches (The Banned List)

### Single Words

**Tier 1 - Immediate rejection:**
```
delve       tapestry      realm         beacon
leverage    synergy       robust        seamless
curate      elevate       empower       foster
resonate    pivot         disrupt       unlock
unleash     harness       revolutionize game-changing
cutting-edge transformative innovative  holistic
```

**Tier 2 - Use extremely sparingly:**
```
journey     powerful      incredible    amazing
passion     authentic     mindful       intentional
optimize    streamline    integrate     dynamic
```

### Phrases

**Death on sight:**
```
"In today's fast-paced world..."
"Let's dive in..."
"Without further ado..."
"At the end of the day..."
"It's important to note that..."
"In terms of..."
"When it comes to..."
"The fact of the matter is..."
"In this day and age..."
"A testament to..."
"Pave the way for..."
"At the forefront of..."
"Push the boundaries of..."
"Embark on a journey..."
"Unlock your potential..."
"Harness the power of..."
"Take your [X] to the next level..."
"We're passionate about..."
"Join our community of..."
"Transform your life..."
```

### Structural Patterns

**AI tells:**
- Starting with "As a [role]..." or "As an [expert]..."
- Three-adjective stacks: "innovative, transformative, and revolutionary"
- Opening with a question immediately answered
- "Imagine a world where..."
- Ending every section with a "key takeaway"
- Bulleted lists of 3-5 vague benefits
- The word "crucial" or "essential" in the first paragraph
- Using "this" as a sentence opener
- "Moreover," "Furthermore," "Additionally" paragraph transitions

## The Quick Smell Test

### Before publishing visuals, ask:

1. Would an AI image generator produce this if given a generic prompt?
2. Does this look like every other wellness/productivity brand?
3. Is there genuine texture and imperfection here?
4. Would this image exist in 2019, or is it peak 2024 AI aesthetic?
5. Can I trace where this visual language came from? (Original inspiration > derivative trend)

### Before publishing copy, ask:

1. Can I find 5 of these words in the "banned list"?
2. Did I start with a cliche opening? (In today's, Welcome to, etc.)
3. Does this sound like corporate LinkedIn or like a human speaking?
4. Would I actually say this out loud to a friend?
5. Run it through an AI detector - if it flags, revise.

## Replacement Guide

| Instead of... | Try... |
|--------------|--------|
| "Unlock your potential" | Name the specific thing they'll do |
| "Embark on a journey" | "Start practicing" or "Begin" |
| "Transformative experience" | Describe what actually changes |
| "Holistic approach" | Name the specific dimensions you address |
| "Leverage the power of" | "Use" |
| "Cutting-edge technology" | Name the actual technology |
| "Passionate about helping" | Show how you help, don't claim passion |
| "Take it to the next level" | Say what "the next level" actually is |
| "In today's fast-paced world" | [Delete. Start with the point.] |
| "Join our community" | "Practice with others" or specific benefit |
